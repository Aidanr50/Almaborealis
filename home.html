Clothes that kids can make

Almaborealis is a knitwear design lab in Edinburgh, Scotland. We create clothing concepts that share traditional craft skills and embrace circular economy design principals, to make products that take in consideration the environment of future generations.

About Us Convertibles

Our Mission

Where children will be the future thinkers, decision makers and citizens. By using earthe’s resources, we, adults, make products for children to use and enjoy. How do these products impact the child when they are a grown up? As adults, we can choose for these products to have a positive, or a negative impact on future generations.

Inspired by theories of Maria Montessori and Friedrich Froebel, combined with circular economy thinking, The Convertibles offer future proof kidswear that holistically considers the end user; the child. From participatory learning activities through to sourcing raw materials and an end-of-life plan of the products, future generations and their world is looked after. Waste becomes no more, and the use natural resources is maximised.
